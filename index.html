UI/UXDeveloper

A UI/UX developer, also known as a front-end developer, is responsible for building the interactive components of websites, applications, and other digital experiences. They focus on creating user interfaces (UI) that are visually appealing and easy to use, while also ensuring a smooth and enjoyable user experience (UX). In essence, they translate design concepts into functional, interactive digital interfaces.
